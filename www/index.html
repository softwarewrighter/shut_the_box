Shut the Box

How to Play Shut the Box

Objective

Flip down all numbered tiles (1-9) to achieve a perfect score of 0!

Game Rules

  • Rolling Dice: When tiles 7, 8, or 9 are still up, roll two dice. When only tiles 1-6 remain, roll one die.
  • Making Moves: Select any combination of upright tiles that sum exactly to your dice total.
  • Scoring: Your final score is the sum of all tiles still standing when the game ends.
  • Game Over: The game ends when no combination of remaining tiles can match your dice roll.

How to Play

  • Click "Roll Dice" to roll the dice
  • Click on numbered tiles to select them (they'll turn yellow)
  • Make sure your selected tiles sum to the dice total
  • Click "Submit Move" to flip down the selected tiles
  • Continue until all tiles are down or no valid moves remain

Strategy Tips

  • Focus on flipping high-value tiles (7, 8, 9) early when possible
  • Look for combinations that use multiple tiles efficiently
  • Plan ahead for the single-die phase when only tiles 1-6 remain
